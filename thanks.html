Friendship

By: Duane K. Anderson

Time roars by in sporadic waves
And life brings treasures in many ways
When pain beats like the heart of sorrow
And you freeze your mind so you won't have to face tomorrow
Smiles can be lost and found again
When our hearts are inflated by the love of a friend

True friends a rare and beautiful mystery
They inspire laughter embedded in honesty
They create memories that last forever
And do things we love to remember
They perforate our heart with euphoric thoughts
That reverberate their love within our hearts
At times when hope fades and dreams ache
We find serenity in the pillow of hope our friends create

Easy to fake but hard to make
True friends host bliss and sweet escape
When I am possessed by sorrow I just take a sip
Of the love encapsulated in our friendship
When our lives fade and our souls take the trip
We will still be bound by a word called "friendship"
